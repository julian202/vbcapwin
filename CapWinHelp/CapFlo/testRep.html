Capillary Flow Porometry Tests Report

After data is recorded and the test is complete, you may analyze the data using the CAPREP Software System. Start CAPREP from within CAPWIN by selecting Execute Report from the CAPWIN Report menu

The report printed for a porometry test consists of a heading, the information shown on the testing setup screen, the mean flow pore pressure and diameter, the bubble point pressure and diameter, and a table of the data gathered during the test.


High Flow/Low Pressure Tests

For samples requiring high flow at low pressures, you may wish to reduce the gas inlet pressure from the air/gas supply. This will reduce the amount of pressure being let into the system at each regulator click. Rerun the Adjust Flow Parameters option in the Calibrate menu. For more informationon this subject refer to the section titled "Adjust Flow Parameters" in the Calibrate section).

To increase the number of data points taken during a test:

  1. Reduce the gas inlet pressure from the air/gas supply
  2. Reduce the size of the sample being tested.
  3. Lower the preginc or v2incr in the Edit Auto Parameters option of the CAPWIN Modify Menu to create smaller steps in the regulator and motorized valve (V2).
