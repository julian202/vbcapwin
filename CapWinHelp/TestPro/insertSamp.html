Overview of insertion of sample into chamber

  1. Place the sample in the bottom of the sample chamber completely covering the bottom O ring.
  2. Install the spacing insert. Be careful not to cover the top opening with the palm of your hand when pressing it down onto the sample. Doing so can cause trapped air to blow fluid out of the pores of wet samples.
  3. Screw the cap onto the chamber. Tighten it to the point where the O ring on top of the spacing insert is compressed against the chamber cap.
  4. Connect the quick-connect fitting to the top of the chamber lid
