General Specifications

The specifications below pertain to Capillary Flow Porometers in general. Your particular configuration may vary.

Sample size:

Between 1.75" and 2.5" diameter (4.5 to 6.25 cm)

Other sizes available with use of adapter plates or external sample holders.

Pressurizing gas:

Clean, dry, compressed air or non-corrosive gas with a pressure of at least 60 psi to a maximum of 110% of the instrument's capacity to the "Gas" inlet of the Porometer.

The capacity is indicated in the model number on the side of the instrument (i.e. CFP-1200-AEX means 1 sample chamber, 200 psi).

Pressure and flow transducers:

Transducers may be included in a variety of configurations. A basic configuration (CFP=1100-A) would include one 100 PSI pressure transducer and one 200 LPM flow meter. A porometer with extended range and accuracy (CFP-1100-AEX) would include a 100 PSI absolute and a 5 PSI differential pressure transducer, and one 200 LPM and one 10 LPM flow meter.

Power requirements:

120 VAC, 60 Hz, or 220 VAC. Custom configurations available.

Maximum Pore Size Range:

60.0 μm - 0.035 μm (Porewick)

Size: 30" H x 19" W x 18½" D
Weight: 100 lb.

Size and weight subject to change
