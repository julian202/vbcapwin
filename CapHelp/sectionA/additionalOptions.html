Additional Options

Envelope Surface Area Text Report
Provides a text report of the Envelope Surface Area test.

Surface Area Graph
Provides a graph of the Surface Area data analysis.

Estimated Fiber Diameter Text Report
Provides a text report of the Estimated Fiber Diameter, which measures the diameter of the individual fibers of a sample, assuming that the fibers are of uniform diameter and length. Generated from Envelope Surface Area data.

Air Flow Resistivity Text Report
Provides a text report of the Air Flow Resistivity.

Pressure Hold Text Report
Text report of Pressure Hold test results.

Pressure Hold Test Graph
Graph of elapsed time versus pressure.

Micro flow Analysis
Looks for low flow by comparing the pressure vs. time. When used for Integrity tests, these graphs shows the flow through the saturated sample, as was shown during the test. These are the only graphs available for the Integrity test.

Text Report
Provides a text report of the Micro flow test analysis.

Graph
Provides a graph report of the Micro flow test analysis.

Temperature vs. Time Graph
Displays a graph of the temperature of the system over the time period of the test.

Pressure and Temperature vs. Time Graph
Displays a graph of both pressure and temperature over time.
